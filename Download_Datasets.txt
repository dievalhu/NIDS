1) Download dataset NSL-KDD from:

https://www.unb.ca/cic/datasets/nsl.html

*Save the files as: KDDTrain.csv and KDDTest.csv

2) Download dataset UNSW-NB15 from:

https://www.unsw.adfa.edu.au/unsw-canberra-cyber/cybersecurity/ADFA-NB15-Datasets/

*Save the files as: UNSWTrain.csv and UNSWTest.csv

3) To generate the KDDPCATrain.csv, KDDPCATest.csv and UNSWPCATrain.csv, UNSWPCATest.csv datasets, run the CORR_PCA_KDD.r and CORR_PCA_UNSW.r scripts, respectively.

*Change the storage location of the datasets.
